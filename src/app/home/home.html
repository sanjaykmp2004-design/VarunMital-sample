About Assurion Services

Welcome to Assurion Services LLP, a specialist consulting firm delivering tailored financial, actuarial and risk management solutions to clients across the financial services sector.  We partner with banks, investment management companies, insurers, pension funds, and fintech companies to help them navigate complex financial landscapes, meet regulatory demands, and drive long-term value.  

With a unique blend of strategic insight and actuarial precision, our team brings deep industry experience and analytical rigor to every engagement. Whether it’s financial modelling, regulatory compliance, capital optimization, or actuarial valuations, we offer solutions that are clear, compliant, and commercially focused. 

At Assurion, we are committed to building lasting partnerships based on integrity, excellence, and results. We serve clients across the globe, combining global best practices with local insights.
