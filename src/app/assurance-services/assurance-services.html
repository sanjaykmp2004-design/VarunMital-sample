Assurance Services

IT Audits & Attestation

Our IT audit and attestation services are designed to evaluate the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of your information systems. We assess and verify the effectiveness of IT controls, ensuring alignment with industry standards and regulatory requirements. Our process enables clients to mitigate risks, improve operational efficiency, and maintain compliance with frameworks relevant to their sector.​

SOC 1, SOC 2, and SOC 3 Reporting

System and Organization Controls (SOC) reports are a cornerstone of trust in today's business environment.

  • SOC 1 reports provide assurance over controls related to financial reporting for service organizations
  • SOC 2 reports evaluate controls pertaining to security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy.
  • SOC 3 reports offer a concise, publicly available summary of an organization's controls

Our team ensures the reporting process is seamless and transparent, enabling organizations to demonstrate compliance and reinforce confidence among clients and regulatory bodies.​

ISO 27001 Audits

We assist organizations in achieving and maintaining ISO 27001 certification, the global standard for information security management systems (ISMS). Our services include readiness assessments, internal audits, and certification support, helping clients establish robust security governance and continuous improvement practices.​

Data Privacy Compliances (GDPR, HIPAA, HITRUST)

Data privacy regulations impose significant responsibilities on organizations. We provide expert guidance and auditing in compliance with GDPR, HIPAA, and HITRUST, helping businesses safeguard sensitive information and maintain trust with stakeholders. Our tailored assessments identify potential gaps and recommend actionable improvements to enable ongoing compliance and risk management.​
