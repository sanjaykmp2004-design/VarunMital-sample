Employee Benefits

We provide end-to-end actuarial support for employee benefit schemes, helping organizations meet statutory, accounting, and strategic requirements under frameworks such as Ind AS 19, IAS 19, AS 15, ASC 715 and US GAAP. Our valuations are designed to be audit-ready, compliant, and tailored to your company's specific needs, whether for financial reporting, due diligence, or internal planning. With deep domain expertise, we assist companies & audit firms in accurately assessing and managing long-term employee liabilities.

Our Services Include:

Gratuity Valuations

Actuarial reports for gratuity liabilities as per statutory and accounting standards.

ESOP Valuations

Fair value assessment of employee stock options using industry-standard models.

Leave Encashment

Valuation of leave liabilities for accurate provisioning and reporting.

Pension Valuations

Detailed pension liability valuations under relevant accounting standards.

Pension Audit Support

Collaboration with CA and audit firms for pension-related audits and disclosures.

Mergers & Acquisitions

Due diligence and liability assessments for employee benefits during transactions.

Insurance Services

We offer specialized actuarial and reporting solutions tailored to the evolving needs of the insurance industry, with a strong focus on regulatory compliance, financial reporting, and model transformation. Our team brings deep technical knowledge and hands-on experience in supporting insurers with financial reporting, new accounting standard implementation (LDTI, IFRS17 etc), actuarial modelling, and ongoing business-as-usual (BAU) reporting. Whether you're looking to build models from the ground up or streamline your existing processes, we provide practical, scalable support aligned with global standards.

Our Services Include:

New Accounting Standard Implementation

Comprehensive support throughout the implementation lifecycle, including gap analysis, testing, and deployment.

Model Development

Design, build, validate, and maintain actuarial models using platforms such as GGY Axis, Prophet, Polysystem, and Python.

Valuation Assistance

Support for quarterly and annual valuation and reporting cycles.

Financial Reporting

Prepare periodic reports, analyze results, and deliver insights.

Model Transformation

Modernize and migrate actuarial models for improved accuracy.
